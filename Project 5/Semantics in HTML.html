Facts about Pandas

Pandas are gentle, bamboo-loving bears native to China, known for
their distinctive black-and-white fur. They play
a vital role in maintaining forest ecosystems by helping spread seeds
and supporting biodiversity. Despite conservation progress, they remain
a symbol of global wildlife protection efforts. Their calm nature and
playful behavior make them loved worldwide. Ongoing conservation programs continue
to support their survival and growth.
